The Malmö Book Club

Us in #läsa (means "to read" in Swedish) form a growing community of booklovers living in Malmö, Sweden. We get together the first Saturday of each month to share our passion for reading.

Check out our planned readings below and if you got interested, sign up! The book club is completely free of charge!

How do we operate?

The purchasing books themselves is the only thing you are responsible for, however, we teamed up with Aldibris to make the books even more accessible for our members! Once you sign up, you'll find your member code in the welcome email we'll send you which will give you 15% off from your purchases from Aldibris.

How do we operate?

  • Meetup the first Saturday of each month at 11:30 am at the Malmö Stadsbibliotek.
  • Our meetings are held in English.
